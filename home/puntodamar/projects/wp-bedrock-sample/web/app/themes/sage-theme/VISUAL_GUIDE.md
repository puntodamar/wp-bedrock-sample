### Creating a Book
1. Click "Add New Book" button
2. Modal slides down
3. Fill in form fields
4. Click "Save Book"
5. Button shows "Saving..."
6. Modal closes
7. Success message appears
8. New book appears in table
9. Message fades after 5 seconds

### Editing a Book
1. Click pencil icon on a book row
2. Modal opens with pre-filled data
3. Modify fields
4. Click "Save Book"
5. Modal closes
6. Table row updates
7. Success message appears

### Deleting a Book
1. Click trash icon on a book row
2. Confirmation dialog appears
3. Click "OK"
4. Row fades out
5. Success message appears

### Closing Modal
- Click X button
- Click outside modal (on overlay)
- Press Escape key
